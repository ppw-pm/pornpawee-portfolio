I'm Pornpawee Manyuen — a UX designer with a deep love for the space where human language meets technology. My background in linguistics gives me a unique lens for understanding how people think, communicate, and interact with systems.

After my Master's in HCID, I've worked across industries — from Southeast Asia's largest OTA to a global social media platform — crafting experiences that are as intuitive as they are meaningful.

I believe great design lives in the details you don't notice: the word choice in a tooltip, the rhythm of an onboarding flow, the moment a user says "oh, that just works."
